# Agentic Research Workflows

Curated research packet on agentic workflows, with emphasis on research workflows, evidence handling, evaluation, and long-horizon applied AI systems.

Cutoff date: March 18, 2026.

This folder contains:

- `sources.md`: primary-source reading list and evidence index
- `workflow_synthesis.md`: how this research should shape AgentOrg's workflow/process

Scope:

- multi-agent research systems
- deep research / deep search systems
- benchmarks for agents, browsing, coding, and scientific work
- context engineering, memory, tool design, and protocol design
- verification, citation grounding, and evaluation integrity

This packet was compiled collaboratively:

- Codex gathered the source base and wrote the synthesis
- Claude contributed a repo-specific prioritization memo that influenced the recommendations in `workflow_synthesis.md`
