# Shifting Tides: The Future of International Alliances

November 14, 2025

Angel Ubide, Managing Director and Head of Economic Research at Citadel, moderated a discussion with Niall Ferguson, Milbank Family Senior Fellow at the Hoover Institution, and Admiral James Stavridis, Vice Chairman of The Carlyle Group and former NATO Supreme Allied Commander. The panel examined the evolving geopolitical and economic order, U.S. strategy under renewed nationalism and the implications for global markets.
