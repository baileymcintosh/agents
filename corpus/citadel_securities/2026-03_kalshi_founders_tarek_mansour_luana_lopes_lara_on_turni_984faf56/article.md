# Kalshi Founders Tarek Mansour & Luana Lopes Lara On Turning Events Into Assets

November 14, 2025

Download the transcript

![Image 1](https://static.thenounproject.com/png/443154-200.png)

Kalshi co-founders Tarek Mansour and Luana Lopes Lara joined Molly O’Shea, host of the Sourcery podcast, for a conversation on how prediction markets are emerging as a new asset class. The discussion covered Kalshi’s rapid growth, its regulatory journey and how event contracts are reshaping finance by turning real-world outcomes like elections, inflation or sports results into tradable assets.
