# Ken Griffin on the Role of the Market Maker Ten Years After the ’08 Financial Crisis

October 15, 2018

We’ve observed a significant evolution of the markets since the financial crisis of 2008. Ten years on, panelists at the Bloomberg Global Business Forum reflected on lessons from the crisis and how the roles of different market participants have shifted. For example, global market makers like Citadel Securities have taken on greater responsibilities for providing liquidity and offering price discovery. In this clip, our Founder Ken Griffin goes into detail about how market makers instill the markets with confidence.
