# Nvidia’s Jensen Huang on AI & the Next Frontier of Growth

November 14, 2025

Nvidia founder and CEO Jensen Huang speaks with Sequoia Capital partner Konstantine Buhler about the company’s journey, from its foundational insight into accelerated computing to its role building the infrastructure for the generative AI revolution. Jensen also details Nvidia’s integrated AI factory platform, the immense opportunities in agentic AI and physical AI, and the importance of sovereign AI.
