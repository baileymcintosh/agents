##### Launched in partnership with Apollo, ATLAS SP Partners provides customized, asset-backed financing and portfolio solutions to help companies fuel innovation and growth.

Watch the video to learn about the ATLAS SP team and the company’s holistic suite of capabilities that support clients throughout growth stages.

**Rob Lecky, _Managing Director, ATLAS SP Partners:_**[ATLAS SP](https://www.atlas-sp.com/) is a specialty finance company and we provide custom services across financing, capital markets, and general advisory business.

**Carey Lathrop, _CEO, ATLAS SP Partners:_**ATLAS SP is a finance company to finance companies. It provides asset-based warehouses and loans. It also provides capital market services, which would be terming out those warehouses into the capital markets.

**Kelly Amen, _Vice President, ATLAS SP Partners:_**With so much industry experience between the entire team, we can really rely on true experts in the industry for their knowledge.

**Maura Miraglia, _Co-Head of Consumer/Commercial Origination, ATLAS SP Partners:_**What’s really unique about ATLAS in particular is just the institutional knowledge we have and the [history](https://www.apollo.com/insights-news/pressreleases/2023/02/apollo-announces-launch-of-atlas-sp-partners-in-connection-with-a-close-of-the-credit-suisse-securitized-products-group-transaction-232310341) of what we’ve been able to do and how creative we get.

**Enrique Flores, _Director, ATLAS SP Partners:_**We have lending, we have structuring, we have distribution. So when you put all of that together, it's a winning combination.

**Cameron Maples, _Vice President, ATLAS SP Partners:_**Everything from banks to start ups to sports, media, entertainment clients.

**Maura Miraglia:** We have a really strong foundation of capital sources. We are really a programmatic solution for our clients. We want to be involved in all aspects of their business.

**Carey Lathrop:** ATLAS capitalizes on the migration of activity away from the banking system. It has deep client relationships and relies on innovation to provide bespoke solutions to clients’ needs.

**Enrique Flores:** We're experts in different asset classes, some of them being commercial and residential real estate, corporate and consumer debt, among other more esoteric asset classes.

**Maura Miraglia:** We really are very, very good at thinking outside the box, working together, finding solutions. And I think that's really where our clients value us as well.

**Kelly Amen:** The sky's the limit for [ATLAS SP](https://www.atlas-sp.com/). I think that with a partner like Apollo, we're going to be able to scale and size our existing business and really have reach to as many clients as possible.

**Carey Lathrop:** Today, Apollo and ATLAS are considered one of the leaders in asset-backed finance.
